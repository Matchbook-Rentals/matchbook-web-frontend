Rental Credit Report Authorization and Disclosure Form

PLEASE READ THIS RENTAL CREDIT REPORT AUTHORIZATION AND DISCLOSURE (“AUTHORIZATION FORM”) CAREFULLY BEFORE CHECKING THE BOX BELOW TO INDICATE YOUR AUTHORIZATION.

This Rental Credit Report Authorization and Disclosure Form (“Authorization Form”) supplements the MatchBook Terms and Conditions (“Agreement”) between MatchBook, LLC (“MatchBook”) and you, the renter applicant (“you”).

By checking the electronic signature consent box below, you authorize MatchBook, in connection with your rental application on the MatchBook platform, to obtain a consumer credit report about you from a consumer reporting agency, in compliance with the federal Fair Credit Reporting Act (“FCRA”), 15 U.S.C. § 1681 et seq., and applicable state laws.

You understand and agree that:

1.      Purpose of Authorization Form. This Authorization Form governs the collection, use, and disclosure of tenant screening information, including information derived from your consumer credit report (“Consumer Report”), by MatchBook for the purpose of evaluating your rental application and, with your express consent, sharing such information with potential landlords or property managers (each, a “Host”).

2.      Information Collected. You understand that your Consumer Report may include, but is not limited to, information regarding credit history; outstanding debts, collections, or charge-offs; loan and credit card applications and inquiries; public records relating to financial judgments; and information reported by lenders, creditors, or financial institutions.

3.      Creation of Derived Tenant Information. You authorize MatchBook to analyze your Consumer Report to create Derived Tenant Information. For purposes of this Authorization Form, “Derived Tenant Information” means information generated by MatchBook from your Consumer Report for tenant-screening purposes. This may include risk assessments, eligibility indicators, or screening categories produced through proprietary models, but does not include disclosure of your full Consumer Report or raw credit score.

4.      Redistribution of Derived Tenant Information. You authorize MatchBook, upon your express consent for each disclosure, to share Derived Tenant Information with potential Hosts for the limited purpose of evaluating your suitability as a tenant. Each disclosure request will identify: (a) the name and contact information of the Host; (b) the property address for which the information is requested; (c) a description of the Derived Tenant Information to be shared; and (d) the date and time of the request. MatchBook will not disclose or redistribute Derived Tenant Information to any other person or entity without your separate, express consent for each such disclosure.

5.      Tenant Rights. You retain the right to:

·         decline any request for redistribution of Derived Tenant Information;

·         revoke your consent under this Authorization Form at any time by providing written notice to MatchBook;

·         request a list of all Hosts who have received your Derived Tenant Information; and

·         dispute the accuracy of any Derived Tenant Information by contacting MatchBook directly.

6.      MatchBook Obligations. MatchBook agrees to:

·         maintain appropriate administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information;

·         use Derived Tenant Information solely for the purpose of facilitating rental applications;

·         comply with the redistribution requirements set forth in Section 4; and

·         comply with all applicable provisions of the FCRA and state consumer privacy laws.

7.      No Other Use. Your Consumer Report and any Derived Tenant Information will be used solely for rental application and renter screening purposes and will not be sold or shared for marketing, advertising, or any unrelated purpose.

8.      Retention. MatchBook and participating Hosts may retain Derived Tenant Information and screening results for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill rental application and record-keeping requirements, subject to applicable law.

9.      FCRA Rights. You understand that you have the right under the FCRA to request a copy of any consumer report obtained about you and to dispute any inaccurate or incomplete information. If any adverse action is taken against you based in whole or in part on information from a Consumer Report, you will receive an Adverse Action Notice explaining the action taken and your rights under the FCRA.

10.  Consent to Receive Electronic Notices. You expressly consent to receive notices electronically by email.
