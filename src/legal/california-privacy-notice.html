Data Retention 

We retain personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including to provide services, comply with legal and regulatory obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and for security and fraud prevention. 

  • Tenant application data is generally retained for 2 years after submission, unless a longer retention period is required by law. 
  • Credit and background information is retained only for as long as required under applicable federal, state, or local laws and securely deleted or de-identified when no longer legally required. 
  • Property listing information remains publicly available on the Website until removed by the host or otherwise deleted in accordance with our policies. 
  • Payment transaction data is retained only as long as necessary to process the transaction and maintain financial records required by law. 
  • Website and technical usage data may be retained for shorter periods, typically 24 months to support analytics, security, and service improvements. 

When personal data is no longer required for these purposes, we will securely delete, de-identify, or anonymize it, in accordance with applicable laws and our data retention policies. 

Notice of No Sale or Sharing of Personal Data 

We do not sell your personal data for monetary consideration. We also do not “share” personal data for cross-context behavioral advertising as those terms are defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). 

We may disclose personal data to service providers and other third parties as described in our Privacy Policy, but such disclosures are made only for business purposes permitted by law, under contracts that limit how the personal data may be used and require the data to be kept confidential. 

Your Privacy Rights 

If you are a resident of California, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Virginia, or other states with similar laws, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. These rights are subject to exceptions and limitations under applicable law. Depending on your state of residence, you may have the right to: 

  • Right to know and access. Request that we disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal data we have collected about you, as well as the categories of sources, purposes for collection, and categories of third parties with whom we disclose that data. 
  • Right to delete. Request that we delete personal data we have collected from you, subject to legal exceptions (for example, where we must retain data for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention, or tenant screening obligations). 
  • Right to correct. Request that we correct inaccurate personal data we maintain about you.  
  • Right to opt out of sale or sharing. Request that we do not “sell” or “share” your personal data, as those terms are defined by law. We do not sell your personal data for monetary consideration, but we may share limited information with service providers or hosts as described in our Privacy Policy. You may opt out of any such sharing at any time.  
  • Right to limit use of sensitive personal information. In California, you may request that we limit our use of sensitive personal information (such as Social Security numbers, government IDs, or financial account numbers) to only those uses necessary to provide services, as permitted by law.  
  • Right to non-discrimination. You have the right not to receive discriminatory treatment from us for exercising your privacy rights. 

How to Exercise Your Rights 

To exercise your rights described above, you may submit a request to us by emailing us at Support@matchbookrentals.com  

To protect your privacy and security, we may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request. We will respond to verified requests within the time period required by applicable law (generally 45 days, with a possible extension if reasonably necessary). 

You may also authorize an agent to make a request on your behalf. If you use an agent, we may require proof of your written authorization and verification of the agent’s identity.
